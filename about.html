ABOUT INCLUVATE

Incluvate, a flagship initiative of the Abled Fitness Foundation, was founded on July 8, 2022, with a bold vision: to create a more inclusive and empowered world for individuals with disabilities in India.

Our journey began with adaptive fitness training, in partnership with AdaptX. Though we certified two individuals through a self-paced program, community research revealed a greater need—livelihood, training, and meaningful employment.

Incluvate has since evolved into a comprehensive platform focused on education, vocational training, internships, placements, and advocacy, empowering individuals with disabilities to live self-reliant, dignified lives.

We are proud associate members of ENIL (European Network of Independent Living), aligning with a global movement and best practices for disability rights and inclusive living.

Mission and Vision

Mission

To empower individuals with disabilities by fostering inclusivity, promoting equal opportunities, and providing access to education, healthcare, and skill development.

Our mission is to uphold the rights and dignity of persons with disabilities by providing accessible education, vocational training, internship and placement opportunities, and guidance for independent living. We advocate for social justice and inclusive policies, fostering a society where all individuals with disabilities can lead empowered and self-reliant lives.

Vision

To create a world where individuals with disabilities live independent, self-reliant, and dignified lives—enabled by inclusive systems, accessible environments, and equal opportunities.

To become the most trusted and inclusive platform empowering individuals with disabilities through education, training, and advocacy—enabling independent living, meaningful employment, and equal participation in society.

Our Founder

Nishanth Kumar Baddam, founder of Incluvate, is a passionate advocate for disability inclusion with firsthand experience. Inspired by challenges he faced both personally and professionally at companies like Google and Amazon, he launched the foundation to promote accessibility and independence. What began as a vision for an adaptive gym has grown into a broader mission of social change. Nishanth now leads efforts to create equal opportunities and foster a more inclusive, barrier-free society.
