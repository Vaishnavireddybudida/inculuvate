Who we Are

At Abled Fitness Foundation (AFF), we are committed to empowering people with disabilities to live with independence, dignity, and purpose. Through a holistic approach, we offer adaptive fitness programs, educational support, skill development, and resources for employment and entrepreneurship.

Our work is rooted in the Independent Living Philosophy and aligned with the UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities. As proud associate members of ENIL (European Network on Independent Living), we’re part of a global movement advocating for equal rights and inclusion.

At AFF, we believe fitness is more than physical—it’s a powerful tool for breaking barriers, building confidence, and transforming lives. We’re creating a world where everyone is valued for their strength, resilience, and potential.

Founder & Director

Abled Fitness Foundation is led by Nishanth Kumar Baddam, a passionate advocate for disability inclusion. As a person with a disability, he deeply understands the challenges faced by the community.

With a strong corporate background at companies like TATA, Cognizant, Google, Sutherland, and Amazon, Nishanth experienced firsthand the gaps in accessibility and support. This inspired him to create change.

Initially envisioning an adaptive fitness center for people with disabilities, Nishanth aimed to establish a specialized gym. Over time, his mission expanded into founding **Abled Fitness Foundation, working toward a barrier-free society where individuals with disabilities can thrive independently.

Driven by personal experience and purpose, Nishanth is committed to creating equal opportunities for all.
